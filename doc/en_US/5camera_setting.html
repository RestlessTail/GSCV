Camera settings

Zoom in & zoom out

By dragging the slider or changing the value directly in the edit box, you can zoom in or zoom out the figure in the viewport.

Note: In consideration of devices with weaker hardware, a strict resource control is applied by default. When dragging the slider, the viewport do not update until the mouse released. If you want the viewport to update when dragging the viewport, you can change its behavior in settings.

Change the size of the points

By dragging the slider or changing the value directly in the edit box, you can modify the size of the points.

Note: In consideration of devices with weaker hardware, a strict resource control is applied by default. When dragging the slider, the viewport do not update until the mouse released. If you want the viewport to update when dragging the viewport, you can change its behavior in settings.

Close this panel

Click the "Close" button, and the panel is closed.
